MyTour Plugin for jQuery


Open-source plugin under MIT license. Written by Rogério Taques

Introduction

This page is a demo of how you can easily create a website tour using jQuery.
When you start the tour, tooltips will be displayed in each step, explaining your website.

Start it now!

Creating a tour

To create your own tour is easy. You just need a few line of HTML and JavaScript.

Describe your site or system

When you can explain your website or web application with a virtual tour it becomes easier and people tends to enjoy more it.


Use the power of "autoplay" and customization

You can easily surprise the visitants of your site, your users or customers with an autoplay tour when they are using your app or visiting your site for the first time! Also, you can fully customize it to integrate the tour (colors, images, etc) with theme on your site or app.
